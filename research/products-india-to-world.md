# India-to-World Products: Export Strategy

## 1. Introduction
This document lists authentic Indian products that have strong international demand but are difficult to access directly from Indian sellers today.

The **India-to-World** module enables global buyers to discover genuine Indian products with **transparent pricing**, **verified sellers**, and **simplified cross-border shipping**.
